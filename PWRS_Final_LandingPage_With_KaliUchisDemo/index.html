What is PWRS?

PWRS is your all-in-one platform for powering artists' careers with precision, data, and strategy.

  • 🧭 Tour Management: Schedule, optimize, and route your entire tour effortlessly.
  • 📈 Revenue Insights: Track earnings by city, venue, and time — visually and interactively.
  • 🗺️ GIT (Global Intelligence Tracking): Explore your career on a globe — past and future tours.
  • 🏆 Progress System: Earn emblems and level up based on milestones and market success.
